Born Jahron Anthony Brathwaite, Canadian artist PARTYNEXTDOOR is one of the industry's most innovative and multifaceted figures. As the first artist signed to OVO Sound, his influence on contemporary R&B has been undeniable. Blending atmospheric production with raw introspection, PARTYNEXTDOOR has shaped a sound that continues to define the evolving landscape of modern music.
